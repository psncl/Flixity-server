## Functional Requirements

- You can search for list of movies by city (shooting location).
- Selecting a city will show a list of 5 or 10 movies matching the criteria.
- The list of movies will contain details of the movie like poster, title, year, director.
- Each movie will have a short blurb about the locations used in the shooting.
- Each movie will have external links to IMDb, Rotten Tomatoes, MUBI, Letterboxd, etc.

## Non-functional Requirements

- Show a clickable list or dropdown of main cities in UK and Europe.
- Show an input field for a custom city.
- The blurb about the locations should be about the particular city selected and be no more than 2-3 lines long.
- Rating should be shown alongside the external links whenever available.
- Display the list of movies in a mansory layout.

  - Individual movies could be shown in a scroll-down view like https://www.nytimes.com/interactive/2026/01/27/well/health-wellness-tips-experts.html
